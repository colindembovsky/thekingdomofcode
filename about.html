Where Tech Meets Faith

The Kingdom of Code is at the Intersection of Faith and Technology.

This is the place where we explore how Christians can navigate technology, innovation, and the digital age with wisdom and purpose.

It is about understanding the role of technology in God's plan, staying Spirit-led in a rapidly changing world, and using our gifts to shape the future rather than fear it. From coding to creativity, ethics to entrepreneurship, and from the digital to the spiritual, we'll uncover how believers can lead in the age of technology.

Join us on this journey as we engage, enlighten, and empower through technology while staying true to our values and faith.

About Colin Dembovsky

Colin Dembovsky is the founder of The Kingdom of Code. He is a Principal Solution Engineer at GitHub, the world's largest developer platform. He has been in the tech industry for over 20 years.

Colin had an encounter with the Holy Spirit when he was 15 and has been a committed believer ever since. He has led worship, small groups and he and his wife attended the Bethel School of Workship in Redding, CA in 2009. Colin has recorded two worship albums (Jika and The Presence Project) and is still actively involved in the worship ministry at his local church.

Colin is originally from South Africa but is now based in Spring, Texas, USA where he lives with his wife and two children, menagerie of pets, 3D printers, drones, cameras, pellet smokers, drums and other instruments and his extensive board game collection.
